The Project

The Sahara Expedition

Most people who come to me are working on behalf of organisations. You're working on behalf of a culture. That's a different kind of brief, and it needs a different kind of approach.

A solo traverse of the Sahara, raising global awareness for the endangered Amazigh culture of North Africa, is not just an adventure. With the last monolingual Amazigh speaker now passed, and climate change reshaping the Sahara in ways that are erasing generations of land knowledge, the urgency of this project is real.

The goal of this work is to make sure that when you reach out to grant bodies and brand partners, the package you put in front of them matches the quality of the project behind it.

The Engagement

How It Works

The Approach

Before you approach anyone, two things need to be true: the case for this expedition needs to be easy to say yes to, and the right people need to be able to find it and recognise what it is. The sprints are built around getting both right, in that order.

01 — Your prep work

Impact to Clarity

Before our call, you'll work through Impact to Clarity — a guided process that surfaces your Theory of Change and gives us both a clear picture of where you're starting from. By the time we speak, the foundations of your thinking are already on paper.

This is your one piece of homework. Everything else is on me.

02 — 60 minutes, online

Alignment Call

We review your Impact to Clarity responses together, get clear on priorities, and lock in the direction for both sprints. This is where you hand it over. You won't need to attend anything after this call.

03 — Two focused half-days, no attendance required

Sprint One and Sprint Two

I block two half-days and do the deep work on your behalf. You get on with your life. Sprint One covers your concept note and brand positioning. Sprint Two covers funding mapping, your calendar, and systems setup.

04 — 30 minutes, online

Handover Call

We regroup to walk through everything that's been built, talk through your next steps, and make sure you feel confident going into your outreach. All deliverables are shared in a single folder before the call. I can also record short Loom walkthroughs of any of the documents if that's helpful.
